Provided services

Accessibility

  • Everything inside our premises is wheelchair accessible (Exhibits, Restrooms, Cafeteria etc).
  • A comprehensive guide written in Braille available in English, German, French and Greek.
  • Museum maps are available at both our entrance hall and here.
  • Sign language tours are available by booking.
  • We provide assistance for visitors requiring extra support.

Experience

  • Guided tours provided by expert lecturers, available for individuals, families, or groups.
  • Multilingual audio tours accessible through our app which you can download here.
  • Our exhibits include interactive hands-on displays for a more immersive and informative experience.
  • Family Activities that include kid-friendly scavenger hunts, play areas and storytelling sessions.

Educational

  • We provide learning classes about art restoration, archaeology and history.
  • Collaboration with schools for educational visits.
  • We host monthly talks by historians, archaeologists and artists.
  • Programs for students and enthusiasts to learn museum operations.

Support

  • Dedicated help-desk for missing items.
  • We provide lockers and coat checks for visitor convenience.
  • For families we provide on-site childcare and activity areas for young children.
  • In case of an emergency we provide well-equipped facilities and trained staff.

Discounts

  • Discounts of 50% for families over 5 children.
  • Discounts of 50% for children under 12 years old.
  • Discounts of 50% for people of university students.
  • Discounts of 50% for people with disability.
